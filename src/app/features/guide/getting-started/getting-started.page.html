Getting Started

Your first steps into the Beyond Horizon universe

What is Beyond Horizon?

Beyond Horizon is an expansive space-based MMO set in a vast universe of competing factions, distant worlds, and limitless possibility. Build your empire across the galaxy, commanding fleets of warships, mining stations, and trade routes.

Engage in epic large-scale battles, form alliances with other players, and leave your mark on the frontier. Whether you're a ruthless tactician, a shrewd merchant, or a frontier explorer, Beyond Horizon offers rich, emergent gameplay where your choices shape the galaxy.

Progression & Ranks

As you grow your empire, your faction rank increases, unlocking access to new ship classes, technology tiers, and strategic bonuses. Early ranks focus on survival and resource gathering; later ranks grant access to devastating capital ships and faction-wide warfare mechanics.

Compete in ranked conflicts, complete objectives, and form powerful alliances to accelerate your rise through the ranks.
