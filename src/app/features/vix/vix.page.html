The galaxy's premier marketplace for fleet acquisition and trade. Powered by Voran Industrial.

The Voran Interstellar Exchange — VIX — is the galaxy's centralized marketplace where commanders buy, sell, and auction their ships. Backed by Voran Industrial's vast network of Imperial Stations, VIX offers secure transactions, automated logistics, and a trading experience worthy of the galaxy's finest commanders.

Whether you're looking to offload surplus ships from your fleet, hunt for a deal on a next-tier destroyer, or build a custom squadron through bundle auctions, VIX is your command center for galactic commerce.

VIX is in addition to the player-run trade base system but it provides a streamlined, empire-backed exchange that handles everything from escrow to delivery.

How VIX Works

For Sellers

List your ships in minutes.

  1. Select Ships — Choose one ship or multiple ships to create a squadron bundle. Any ship in your fleet can be listed.
  2. Set Your Price — Choose a starting bid and optionally set a Buy It Now price for instant sales. Prices must fall within fair market guardrails (minimum 25% of build cost, maximum 5x build cost).
  3. Choose Duration — Pick how long your auction runs: 1 hour, 3 hours, 6 hours, 12 hours, or up to 24 hours.
  4. Voran Handles the Rest — Once you confirm your listing, Voran dispatches an invulnerable carrier to transport your ships to the nearest Imperial Station. Your listing goes live the moment they arrive.

When your ship sells, Voran deposits the proceeds directly into your account (minus a small broker fee). It's that simple.

For Buyers

Find your next flagship.

  1. Browse & Search — Filter by ship type, tech tier, price range, and more. Watch auctions that catch your eye.
  2. Bid Smart — VIX uses a proxy bidding system. Set your maximum bid and VIX automatically bids on your behalf, only going as high as needed to stay in the lead. Your maximum bid is never revealed to other players.
  3. Or Buy It Now — See something you must have? Hit Buy It Now for an instant purchase (available only before the first bid is placed).
  4. Delivery on Your Terms — Won an auction? Tell Voran where to deliver. Choose your system, your base, or even a specific planet for ground units. Voran's carriers handle the rest, and you can track your delivery in real-time.

What Makes VIX Different

Proxy Bidding

Set your max and walk away. VIX bids for you automatically, keeping you in the lead without overpaying. Your maximum bid stays secret.

Squadron Bundles

Sell entire fleets as a package deal. Bundle any combination of ships into a single listing — fighters, frigates, capital ships, you name it.

Secure Escrow

Every bid is backed by real credits. When you bid, your credits are held safely in escrow. If you're outbid, they're returned instantly. No more worrying about buyers who can't pay.

Anti-Snipe Protection

No more last-second steals. If a bid is placed in the final seconds of an auction, the timer automatically extends by one minute, giving everyone a fair chance to respond.

Real-Time Tracking

Watch your ships travel across the galaxy. From the moment a Voran carrier picks up your purchase to the moment it docks at your base, you can track every jump in real-time.

Seller Ratings

Build your reputation. After every completed sale, buyers can rate their experience. Top-rated sellers earn trust — and more bids.

Buy & Sell Directly from Voran

Not every transaction needs an auction. VIX also operates a direct shop where you can buy ships and resources straight from Voran Industrial.

Buying Ships from Voran

Purchase any ship you've unlocked (up to one tech tier above your current completed research) at 150% of the standard build cost. No waiting for auctions, no bidding wars — just instant acquisition with delivery to your chosen location.

Selling Ships to Voran

Need quick credits? Sell any ship from your fleet directly to Voran for 50% of its build cost. The transaction is instant — credits deposited immediately.

Resource Trading

Buy raw resources at market price or sell your surplus to Voran at 50% value. Need resources delivered to a specific warehouse? Voran's cargo fleet will handle it for an additional delivery fee.

A Fair Market for Everyone

VIX is designed to be a fair, balanced marketplace. Here's how we keep it that way:

Price Guardrails

Starting bids cannot be set below 25% of a ship's build cost, and Buy It Now prices cannot exceed 5x the build cost. This prevents both dumping and price gouging.

Listing Limits

Each commander can have a maximum of 10 active listings in any 24-hour period. This keeps the market diverse and prevents any single player from flooding the exchange.

Escrow System

All bids are backed by held credits. You cannot bid with money you don't have. Period.

All Bids Are Final

Once a bid is placed, it stands. Sellers can cancel a listing, but only with a heavy penalty and never in the final 15 minutes.

Invulnerable Transit

Ships in transit under Voran's care cannot be attacked or intercepted. Your assets are safe from listing to delivery.

Voran Tokens — Command the Market

Voran Tokens (VT) are premium tokens that unlock special perks within VIX. Purchase Voran Tokens through the in-game store.

Instant Delivery

Skip the wait. Apply a Voran Token to any delivery and your ships appear at their destination immediately. No transit time.

Featured Listings

Make your listing stand out. Featured listings appear at the top of search results with a highlighted border and special badge, grabbing more attention from buyers.

Reduced Broker Fees

Apply a Voran Token to reduce your broker fee on your next 5 listings. Pay less, keep more of your profits.

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I win an auction but forget to set a delivery location? {{ openFaq() === 0 ? '−' : '+' }}

You have 48 hours after winning to provide delivery instructions. After that, your ships will be held at the Imperial Station where the auction took place. You can manually retrieve them at any time.

Can I cancel a bid? {{ openFaq() === 1 ? '−' : '+' }}

Bids are final and cannot be cancelled. Make sure you're comfortable with your maximum bid before placing it.

What happens if my auction gets no bids? {{ openFaq() === 2 ? '−' : '+' }}

You can relist it for free (no additional listing fee) or retrieve your ships. You have 24 hours to decide. If you don't act, Voran will return the ships to you with a delivery cost deducted.

Can I sell ships I haven't researched yet? {{ openFaq() === 3 ? '−' : '+' }}

You can only list ships you own. You can buy ships from Voran Direct Shop up to one tier above your completed research, but you cannot purchase ships two or more tiers above.

How does proxy bidding work? {{ openFaq() === 4 ? '−' : '+' }}

When you place a bid, you set a maximum amount you're willing to pay. VIX then bids on your behalf automatically, only increasing your visible bid as needed to stay ahead of other bidders. You'll never pay more than necessary, and your maximum is never revealed.

Is there a minimum number of ships for a bundle? {{ openFaq() === 5 ? '−' : '+' }}

Bundles require at least 2 ships. There is no maximum — sell your entire fleet in one listing if you want.

Can I message a seller before bidding? {{ openFaq() === 6 ? '−' : '+' }}

Yes. Every listing has a "Message Seller" button where you can ask questions about the ship's specs, history, or condition.

What are Voran Tokens? {{ openFaq() === 7 ? '−' : '+' }}

Voran Tokens are a premium currency available through the in-game store. They unlock convenience perks like instant delivery, featured listings, and reduced broker fees. They do not affect gameplay balance — just convenience and visibility.

How is seller rating calculated? {{ openFaq() === 8 ? '−' : '+' }}

After a completed delivery, the buyer can rate the seller from 1 to 5 stars with an optional review. Your seller profile shows your average rating and total number of completed sales.
