How to Play

Beyond Horizon is an expansive space-based MMO set in a vast universe of competing factions, distant worlds, and limitless possibility. Build your empire across the galaxy, commanding fleets of warships, mining stations, and trade routes. Engage in epic large-scale battles, form alliances with other players, and leave your mark on the frontier.

Whether you're a ruthless tactician, a shrewd merchant, or a frontier explorer, Beyond Horizon offers rich, emergent gameplay where your choices shape the galaxy.

Create Your Account: Sign up on the Beyond Horizon launcher or website to begin your journey.

Choose Your Faction: Align with one of three rival factions, each with unique ship designs and strategic advantages.

Claim Your System: Establish your first station and begin mining resources, building ships, or engaging in commerce.

Join the Battle: Form alliances, engage in large-scale fleet warfare, and compete for dominance across the galaxy.

As you grow your empire, your faction rank increases, unlocking access to new ship classes, technology tiers, and strategic bonuses. Early ranks focus on survival and resource gathering; later ranks grant access to devastating capital ships and faction-wide warfare mechanics.

Compete in ranked conflicts, complete objectives, and form powerful alliances to accelerate your rise through the ranks.

Beyond Horizon thrives on player interaction. Form guilds, coordinate massive fleet operations, negotiate trade agreements, and wage wars across shared star systems. The game world evolves based on player decisions—your actions matter.
